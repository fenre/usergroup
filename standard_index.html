About Us

The Oslo Splunk User Group (OSUG) is a community for professionals and enthusiasts using Splunk for observability, security, IT operations, and more. We aim to share knowledge, best practices, and experiences.

Whether you're a seasoned Splunk architect, a developer instrumenting applications, or a sysadmin managing infrastructure, join us for meetups, presentations, and discussions.
